JustSmoothie has built its identity on a basic rule of nutrition: fresh produce can not be consumed all year round. That's why the card lives at the rhythm of the seasons and is renewed every three months. From kefta dumplings to pastrami sandwich, to naan, Season offers a cuisine open to the world, healthy and revisited with a touch of french touch.
